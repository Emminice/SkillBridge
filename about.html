About SkillBridge

Connecting talent with opportunity through innovative technology and human connection.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, SkillBridge was born out of a simple observation: the traditional job market wasn't working for everyone. Skilled professionals struggled to find clients, while businesses and individuals had difficulty finding reliable service providers.

Our founders, a team of entrepreneurs and technologists, set out to create a platform that would bridge this gap - hence the name SkillBridge. What started as a small project quickly grew into a global marketplace connecting millions of professionals with clients in need of their services.

Today, SkillBridge operates in over 180 countries, facilitating connections across hundreds of service categories from plumbing and catering to web development and graphic design.
